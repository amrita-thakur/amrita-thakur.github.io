Associate Data Scientist - GritFeat Solutions

Duration: May 2021 - Nov 2021

    Risk Stratification System

    I developed a Risk Stratification System using claims data on the AWS SageMaker platform. The objective was to identify patients who require enhanced care proactively, allowing the organization to provide better treatment and care plans from the outset. This approach aimed to avoid prolonged treatments and minimize overall costs.

    Utilizing historical claims data that adhered to ICD-10 codes, we focused heavily on feature engineering, analyzing various features related to procedures, diagnoses, and medications. A significant challenge arose from the one-hot encoding of this data, which led to numerous sparse matrices. To address this, we employed Principal Component Analysis (PCA) for effective feature extraction, ensuring we maximized the value derived from our dataset.

    After experimenting with various supervised machine learning algorithms, we determined that Gradient Boosting was the most effective model for this application, yielding the best results in risk stratification.

    Core Competencies: Python, USA Healthcare Claims Data, AWS (S3, SageMaker).
